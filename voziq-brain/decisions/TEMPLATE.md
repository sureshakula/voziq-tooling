# NNNN: Title

## Context

What situation forced a choice. Include the constraints that were real at the time (deadlines, team size, client commitments), because they're what make the decision make sense later.

## Decision

What we chose, stated plainly in one or two sentences.

## Alternatives we rejected

Each with the one-line reason it lost. This section is what saves the next person from re-proposing them.

## Consequences

What this costs us, what it buys us, and what would have to change for us to revisit it.
