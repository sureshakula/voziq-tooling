# Decisions

Architecture decision records: what we chose, what we knew at the time, and what it costs us. The audience is the engineer two years from now asking "why on earth is it built this way," who deserves a better answer than git blame.

Write one when a choice is made that someone will later question: a technology pick, a data-model shape, a process rule, a deliberate rejection of the obvious approach.

Copy `TEMPLATE.md`, number it sequentially, keep it under a page. A decision record that takes more than five minutes to read won't be read.

Statuses: `proposed` (PR open, not yet agreed), `accepted`, `superseded` (link to what replaced it).
