# Runbooks

Procedures you'll run more than once: deploys, backfills, rotations, recoveries, client onboarding steps. A good runbook lets someone who has never done the task do it correctly at 2am without paging you.

Copy `TEMPLATE.md`. The verification and rollback sections are not optional; a runbook without them is a suggestion, not a procedure.

When you follow a runbook and hit something it didn't cover, PR the fix into the runbook before you forget. The person who just ran it is the only person who knows it's wrong.
